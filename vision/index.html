Vision

My life goal is to advance humanity by techonology with philosophy. At the end of the day, we, including me, will die. But I believe the fact that we made something valuable for us, humanity, and our predecessors will be long-lasting as it's worthwhile. So, I believe contributing to humanity's advance is the essential and meaningful direction I should have. Technology is magic. It has changed the world. I think the next big thing will be Brain-Machine Interfaces. When we can directly connect to a cutting-edge computer at any time, that will accelerate everything and help bring humanity to the final stage. I will be the "fierce nerd" to lock myself in a room and work on something that matters.

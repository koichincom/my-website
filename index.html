Clubs

I'm leading two clubs, unofficial yet. If you're a De Anza student, and interested in technology, even if you're a beginner, feel free to join us!

  • DAHCI (De Anza Human-Computer Interface): We explore Human-Computer Interaction within Cognitive Science, utilizing techniques like heart rate monitoring, eye tracking, and EEG.
  • MLCL (Machine Learning for College Life): Our goal is to bridge gap between Machine Learning and everyday college life, making technology more accessible and beneficial to students.

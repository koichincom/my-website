To De Anza Students

I'm leading two unofficial clubs at De Anza College. If you're interested in technology—whether you're a beginner or not— feel free to join us. Open to De Anza students only.

  • DAHCI
    (De Anza Human-Computer Interaction) We explore Human-Computer Interaction within Cognitive Science, utilizing techniques like heart rate monitoring, eye tracking, and EEG.
  • MLCL
    (Machine Learning for College Life) Our goal is to bridge the gap between Machine Learning and everyday college life, making technology more accessible and beneficial to students.

To DA Students

To De Anza students: I'm leading two unofficial clubs. If you're interested in technology —whether you're a beginner or experienced—feel free to join us.

  • DAHCI (De Anza Human-Computer Interface)
    We explore Human-Computer Interaction within Cognitive Science, utilizing techniques like heart rate monitoring, eye tracking, and EEG.
  • MLCL (Machine Learning for College Life)
    Our goal is to bridge the gap between Machine Learning and everyday college life, making technology more accessible and beneficial to students.
